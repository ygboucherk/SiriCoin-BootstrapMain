About Us

Short paragraph on the birth of SiriCoin

We SiriCoin Devs at first met up at a crypto currency discord server and we didnt know much about cryptos that time and we also didnt know how to code.. One fine day we all got demoted and banned from that cryptocurrency server, From there onwards we started to make our own crypto currency.

And since we didnt know how to code and make crypto we began to visit a number of different cryptos that can be mined on android phones and avrs, but we couldnt find any of them which were decentralised, also mineable on low powered devices. So we learnt Coding and came to a final desicion of making SiriCoin which can be mined on low powered devices..
